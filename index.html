FAQ

  • Do I need to let the club know before I show up for the first time?
    No. Anytime the club is meeting, you are welcome to stop by at your convenience.
  • How do I know what's going on at the club this week?
    Please see our Facebook Group for events and updates.
  • Do I need to bring my own chess set and/or clock?
    Bringing your own set and clock is encouraged, but not required.
  • Are beginners welcome?
    Yes! Everyone is welcome, all skill levels and ages.
